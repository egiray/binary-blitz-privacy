Binary Blitz - Support

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Need help? This page covers how to play, frequently asked questions, and troubleshooting tips. For bug reports or feature requests, see the Contact section below.

1. About Binary Blitz

Binary Blitz is a fast-paced mathematical puzzle game where you combine powers of 2 to match random targets. Test your mental math skills and progress through 20 challenging tiers!

2. How to Play

  1. Select numbers from the grid by tapping them
  2. Match the target shown at the top by adding your selected numbers
  3. Complete levels before the timer runs out
  4. Progress through tiers by reaching higher levels

Game Rules

  • All numbers are powers of 2 (1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, etc.)
  • Selected numbers turn pink with a checkmark ✓
  • The green check button (✓) activates when your sum matches the target
  • Timer decreases as you advance through tiers
  • You can pause after completing a level using the pause button (||)

3. Scoring & Progression

How Scoring Works

  • Exceed target level: Gain points (+5 per level above target)
  • Fall short: Lose points (-5 per level below target)
  • Your cumulative score determines your tier (1-20)

What Are Tiers?

Tiers represent your skill level. Higher tiers:

  • Start at more difficult levels
  • Have faster timers
  • Require reaching higher target levels
  • Feature larger numbers and more complex combinations

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pause the game?

Yes! After completing a level, tap the pause button (||) in the upper-right corner. You can choose to:

  • Resume: Continue playing immediately
  • Main Menu: Save your progress and return to the main menu (you can resume later)

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings from the main menu. Binary Blitz supports 8 languages:

  • English
  • Spanish (Español)
  • French (Français)
  • Portuguese (Português)
  • Russian (Русский)
  • German (Deutsch)
  • Turkish (Türkçe)
  • Italian (Italiano)

How do I turn off sound or haptics?

Open Settings from the main menu and toggle the Sound or Haptics switches.

The tutorial shows every time I start the game

Complete the tutorial fully (all the way through) once, and it won't show again. The tutorial only appears for first-time players or if you have a saved game.

Why do I see ads?

Binary Blitz is free to play and supported by ads. Ads appear between games (after the timer runs out) and help us keep the game free for everyone.

5. Troubleshooting

Game runs slowly or freezes

  1. Force quit the app completely
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. Free up device storage (game needs approximately 50MB)

Sound doesn't work

  • Check device volume and mute switch (especially on iPhone)
  • Verify Sound is enabled in Settings
  • Try toggling Sound off and on in Settings

Haptic feedback doesn't work

  • Ensure Haptics is enabled in Settings
  • Note: Some older devices don't support haptic feedback
  • Check if haptics work in other apps

My progress/score was lost

Game data is stored locally on your device. Progress can be lost if:

  • App data was deleted (clearing app cache/data)
  • Device was restored without backup
  • You switched devices (progress doesn't sync between devices)

Prevention tip: Back up your device regularly using iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) to preserve app data.

Ads not loading or showing errors

  • Ensure you have internet connectivity
  • Ads will be skipped automatically if they fail to load (you can still continue playing)
  • Try restarting the app

Game won't rotate to landscape

Binary Blitz only works in landscape orientation. Make sure:

  • Rotation lock is disabled on your device
  • You've physically rotated your device to landscape

6. Technical Requirements

  • Platform: iOS 13.0+ / Android 6.0+
  • Orientation: Landscape only
  • Storage: Approximately 50MB
  • Internet: Required for ads only (gameplay works offline)

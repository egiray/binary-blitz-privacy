Privacy Policy for Binary Blitz

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: Binary Blitz uses third-party services for analytics, advertising, and crash reporting. We do not collect personal information directly, but these services may collect device and usage data as described below.

1. Overview

Binary Blitz ("the App") is a mobile puzzle game developed by Erdem Giray. This privacy policy explains how we and our third-party partners handle information in our App.

2. Information We Collect

Binary Blitz does NOT directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, we use third-party services that may automatically collect certain data:

Data Type Purpose Service
Usage Data Analytics Firebase Analytics
Advertising ID Personalized Ads Google AdMob
Crash Data Bug Fixing Sentry

3. Third-Party Services

Google Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players use our App. This helps us improve gameplay and fix issues.

Data collected:

  • App usage events (game starts, level completions, tier changes)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • General location (country/region, not precise location)
  • App performance data

Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

We display interstitial advertisements between games using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data to show relevant ads.

Data collected:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

Opt-out: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Sentry

We use Sentry for crash reporting and error tracking to help us identify and fix bugs quickly.

Data collected:

  • Crash logs and error messages
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App state at time of crash

Privacy Policy: Sentry Privacy Policy

4. Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (tier, score, current level)
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptics, language)
  • Saved game state (if you pause mid-game)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can delete this data by uninstalling the App.

5. Permissions

Binary Blitz requests minimal permissions:

  • Screen Orientation: To lock the display to landscape mode
  • Internet Access: Required for displaying ads and sending analytics/crash data
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback (can be disabled in settings)

6. Children's Privacy

Binary Blitz is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The third-party services we use (Firebase, AdMob, Sentry) have their own policies regarding children's data.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you uninstall the App
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase according to their data retention policies (typically 14 months)
  • Crash data: Retained by Sentry for 90 days
  • Ad data: Managed by Google according to their privacy policy

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about data collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable ad tracking and analytics via device settings

To exercise these rights regarding third-party services, please refer to their respective privacy policies linked above.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data processed through our App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Our third-party partners maintain their own security practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the new policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
